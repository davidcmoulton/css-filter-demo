Filters

Note that drop-shadow() and url() filters are omitted from this list. drop-shadow() is similar to box-shadow, but you can't use inset nor provide any value for spread. It might get some hardware acceleration, depending on the browser. url() references an SVG filter, and as such is out of scope.
